Make an Appointment

Three ways to get your name on out dily wait list:

  1. Fill out the contact form bellow and let us know when would you like to come
  2. Simply call the shop, find out if the estimated wait time works for you and if it does... we'll add your name to the daily wait list by phone.
  3. Stop in and see what it's all about. We recognize your time is valuable, so you are not required to wait at the shop if you have other things to accomplish during the day. We will personally call you in 10-15 minutes.
